Metaphorical Cards "Petals of You" — MAK cards and self-reflection tools from Petals.digital

"Petals of You" — an author's deck of art-metaphorical cards (MAK cards), developed as a self-reflection tool and psychological work. The deck contains 31 state cards with original metaphorical images, intervention scenarios and a system of ecological questions.

These psychological cards are suitable for daily self-practice and for use by psychologists, coaches and art therapists. Unlike typical MAK cards, the deck does not contain ready interpretations — it helps see your own state through image and precise question.

Author — Yavtushenko Yulia, creator of the self-reflection tools system Petals.digital. The deck is available in digital format (PDF, 890 UAH) and as a physical deck (1200 UAH). Free art therapy guide is available on petals.digital.
